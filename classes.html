Art Classes in Vinings

Add your own art to your art collection by joining a class here at the Vinings School of Art. You can also learn a new language and/or instrument. Reach out to us today.

Know Before you Register

  • $10 once in a lifetime registration fee (not applicable to one time $20 drop-in ART if space available)
  • $2 transaction fee for using credit cards (NOTE debit cards or checks or online payment no additional fee)
  • Children pay for 4 consecutive weeks NO MONTHLY fee because most months have less than 4 classes
  • PRIVATE PIANO, VOCAL, GUITAR, ½ hour $33 or $120 for four ½ hr lesson (1 student or 2 siblings)
