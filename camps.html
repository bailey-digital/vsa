Art classes, camps and parties for all ages

Our Vinings art school has been offering art classes for all ages and camps since 2006.

You can now book Fall classes for children and Fall classes for adults! Our schedule was updated so please refresh your screens!

The Vinings School of Art (VSA) opened in April 2006 and is located at the corner of Cumberland Parkway and South Atlanta Road in the IVY WALK plaza.

Here at VSA, we celebrate all of the arts by offering Ô¨Åne arts such as drawing, painting, sculpture-making and clay-work, music eduation and learning to play the piano or play guitar, and Spanish language arts (language study) in one hour classes, 90 minute classes and school holiday full day camps for children.

Although we offer many adult level classes, we believe in the importance of nurturing the artistic talents of children, starting as young as 2 years old!

Classes

We have offered classes for children and for adults in the Fine Arts (drawing, painting, sculpture-making, clay work), Spanish Language Arts (different levels of Spanish language study), and Music Education for Piano, Guitar, Ukulele and Vocal lessons.

Camps

We teach children and teens how to paint canvas paintings, make and grout mosaics, use wood and plaster for sculpture-making, create clay items with different types of clay, work with charcoal and learn special drawing techniques, etc.

Parties

Whether you want us to teach a canvas painting, or make a painted 3-dimensional sculpture out of wood and plaster of Paris, some type of painting on a wood plaque, or clay jewelry-making that you create and paint, we will design a "one of a kind" art project or you can choose from the sample project display table.
