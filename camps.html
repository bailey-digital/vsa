Know Before you Register

  • All fees are non-refundable.
  • No cancellations. Re-scheduling fee to change weeks if at least 14 days prior to your camp start date $30 per student per camp.
  • Register in advance for:
    • early drop off and it is $40 per 5 day week per student.
    • late pick up between 3:01pm and 4:10pm and it is $40 per 5 day week per student.
    • latest pick up between 4:11pm to 5:10pm and it is $40 + $40 = $80 per 5 day week, otherwise it is $20 per day child

Art Camps all year round

Winter Break Art Camps

December 19, 20, 21, 2022 art camp spots available!

We have 3 classrooms in our facility and separate age groups. Each day is unique: we will do one or two different pieces of art each day with canvas paintings, clay work, drawing techniques, or 3d sculpture-making with wood, paint, and plaster. Weather permitting, students will have the option of an outdoor snack and lunch time on our tables on the outside patio.

Summer Break Art Camps

These are ART camps for age 6-8yrs., 8-10yrs., and 10-14yrs. Optional early drop-off starts at 8am and optional later pick up, although the main camp hours are drop off 9:15am with pick up 2:45pm to 3pm. Students must bring their own food and beverage items. We teach students how to paint large size canvas paintings, drawing techniques, clay camp, mosaic art with tiles grouted into wood trays, and 3 dimensional sculpture-making. Each week is unique.
