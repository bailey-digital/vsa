Painting Parties for Birthdays or Private Events

Age 5 and up: We would love to host your private painting party!
We have party times available December 12-18, 2022, only.

The Vinings School of Art has been running painting parties since 2006. Whether you want us to teach a canvas painting, or make a painted 3-dimensional sculpture out of wood and plaster of Paris, some type of painting on a wood plaque, or clay jewelry-making that you create and paint, we will design a "one of a kind" art project or you can choose from the sample project display table. We also host special events like Ladies Night Out, bridal showers and corporate team outings. All art supplies are included for the prices listed below, and you will need to bring your own food and beverage items.

Party includes

  • Private room and NO other birthday parties at our facility during your party
  • Art instructor(s) to teach everyone
  • Art supplies for all the participants
  • Decorations
  • $100 non-refundable deposit/down payment, and pay the balance the day of the party

Sample 2 Hour Party

Additional charge for 3-hour party

  • 5-10 minutes for guest arrival, greetings, putting on smocks
  • 70 to 90 minutes for your art class
  • 20-35 minutes for your food, drink and cake
  • 5 minutes to thank everyone and say bye!
