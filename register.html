Classes

We have offered classes for children and for adults in the Fine Arts (drawing, painting, sculpture-making, clay work), Spanish Language Arts (different levels of Spanish language study), and Music Education for Piano, Guitar, Ukulele and Vocal lessons.

Camps

We teach children and teens how to paint canvas paintings, make and grout mosaics, use wood and plaster for sculpture-making, create clay items with different types of clay, work with charcoal and learn special drawing techniques, etc.
